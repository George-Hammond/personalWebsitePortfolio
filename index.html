About me.

Hi! I'm George Hammond, A young ambitious full stack developer from Ghana. I have a masters degree in Business Adminitration from the University of Bolton (UK) and a Bachelor degree in Computer Science from the University of Cape Coast in Ghana.

Growing up in Accra, Ghana, I had an early childhood exposure to problem solving and thinking. I also learnt to transform obstacles into opportunities, giving careful thought to the impact of my decisions on all stakeholders in both my social and professional relationships and responsibilities. Though my life has not been without challenges and obstacles,I have always managed to make the best out of each situation. Besides, storms make able navigators.

I enjoy writing and listening to music, during which I am able to reflect on my daily career goals.
